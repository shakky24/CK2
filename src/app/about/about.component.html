About Us

Cinema Kompany, established in 2011 by Harikrishnan R and Creative Director Avinash G Nair, has built a solid reputation as a leader in movie promotions. Formerly known as BOOM Cinemaz, our company has played a pivotal role in the successful promotion of over 18 films across the Malayalam and Tamil.

In 2024, we entered a new era by merging with DIB Global Entertainments, a strategic move that led to our rebranding as Cinema Kompany. As a part of the DIB Global family, we continue to push the boundaries of innovation in the entertainment industry while upholding our legacy of excellence in film promotion.

Our expertise now extends beyond traditional promotions. Cinema Kompany is set to venture into the OTT space with the launch of our YouTube entertainment channel in mid-2025. This platform will showcase a diverse range of content, from short films to unplugged performances, all under the creative guidance of renowned writer Arun Sukumaran Nair.

Additionally, Cinema Kompany is a prominent player in movie charting for theaters in Kollam, with ongoing expansion across Kerala, further solidifying our position in the regional entertainment landscape.

At Cinema Kompany, we remain dedicated to delivering high-impact promotional services and creating opportunities for captivating storytelling. Our commitment to professionalism and innovation has earned us the trust of filmmakers and audiences alike.
